About Us

Our Site

Whisk Network is a comprehensive website where you can upload cooking/baking recipes and view the work of others! Our objective is to give users a place to post their favorite recipes so that others can prepare and enjoy them as well.

We decided to name our site Whisk Network because we wanted a place where not only we can upload recipes but so can all of our users. This way the recipes on this site can be a combination of multiple different cultures and styles.

Our Team

We are two college students names Brianna Tilton and Hannah Baker. We are Information Technology majors at Southern New Hampshire University. We are working on a project for our Web Based Design final which is a functional website. We chose to do a recipe based website because we both love cooking/baking recipes.
