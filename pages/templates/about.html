About Us

Our Site

Whisky Network is a comprehensive website where you can upload cooking items and view the work of others! Our objective is to give users a place to post your favorite recipes so that others can prepare and enjoy them as well. Whisky Network also provides a way for everyone to easily adapt a recipe to the size that best suits their needs.

We decided to name our site Whisk Network because we wanted a place where not only we can upload recipes but so can all of our users. This way the recipes on this site can be a combination of multiple different cultures and styles.

Our Team

We are two college students names Brianna Tilton and Hannah Baker. We are Information Technology majors at Southern New Hampshire University. We are working on a project for our Web Based Design final website. We chose to do a recipe based website because we both love cooking/baking recipes.
